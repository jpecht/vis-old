Fantasy Basketball Ranks 2016
the top basketball players in fantasy scoring for 2016
Oct 21, 2016

So for the 2016-2017 season, I decided to a repeat of an analysis I did two years ago (mostly because I'm in a league this season =P).

It's a list of the NBA players ranked by how well they performed in the 8 categories used for fantasy basketball. Players are ranked in each category relative to how well they perform among their peers. This makes sense because in fantasy basketball, you want to pick players that not only score points, but perform well in every category.

The number shown for each player represents the sum of that player's Z-value for each category using a normal distribution. The pool used were the top 200 players in fantasy points last season. Also, the turnover category was omitted this season.

So, a positive score indicates above average performance, while a negative score indicates below average performance.
