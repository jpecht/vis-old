Education Spending
how much money is spent per student in the u.s.
Sept 16, 2014
A map showing how much money is spent on education across the states. Blue counties spend more on education, while red counties spend less. The categories are divided into money spent on instructors and money spent on "support" (i.e. administration). The data was taken "per student" to avoid population size playing an influence. All data is comparative to the national average.
