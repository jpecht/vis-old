Counter-Strike Maps
a look at the balance in various counter-strike maps
Nov 17, 2014

This post is on one of my favorite games, Counter-Strike, specifically the newest one, Global Offensive.

Looking at the chart, it's interesting to see how the rounds become more CT-sided as the players' skill level increases. My guess is that defending bomb sites becomes easier the more familiar the players are with the map and the game's mechanics.

It doesn't surprise me that nuke, inferno, and train are all CT-sided. Those are all maps that can be held easily with good CT coverage. Aztec, dust, and vertigo are of course incredibly CT-sided, which is why the maps are never in the competitive circuit. I haven't played the new cbble so not much to say on that. Other than that, overpass looks promising balance-wise.

And of course, de_dust2 still remains the most balanced map in CS history. =D
