How Teams Do In March Madness
NCAA Basketball tournament performance
Mar 19, 2015
Below is a table of the 10 teams that have performed the best and the 10 teams that have performed the worst in the NCAA March Madness tournament in the last 30 years.
Teams are ranked by comparing the team's seed number with how far they advanced in the tournament. Teams get a point for every game they win in the tournament, but receive point deductions for being a higher seed. The scoring goes by the following rules:
• 9-16 seeds (-0) are expected to lose in the First Round (+0)
• 5-8 seeds (-1) are expected to get to the Round of 32 (+1)
• 3-4 seeds (-2) are expected to get to the Sweet Sixteen (+2)
• 2 seeds (-3) are expected to get to the Elite Eight (+3)
• 1 seeds (-4) are expected to get to the Final Four (+4)
• No team is expected to get to the National Championship (+5)
• No team is expected to win the National Championship (+6)
