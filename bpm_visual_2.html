Sep 14, 2016

So now that I've had 2 years worth of new music since I've last done this (mostly been EDM), I wanted to re-analyze my music collection. This time though, I shrunk the BPM range to 78-155 instead of the previous 58-200. This makes more sense and songs outside the range are doubled/halved as appropriate.

I'm also collating the data beforehand instead of making you load the entire 11mb of xml. :)

* curve being drawn is a b-spline
